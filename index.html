About Our Bakery

Welcome to Nthabie's Sweets, where every bite tells a story of passion and craftsmanship.

Founded in the heart of Pretoria East, we've been delighting customers with our handmade baked goods since 2018. Our founder, Nthabie, started baking as a hobby and turned her passion into a thriving business that serves the entire Gauteng region.

We use only the finest ingredients, locally sourced whenever possible, to create treats that are as beautiful as they are delicious. Each product is made with meticulous attention to detail and a whole lot of love.
